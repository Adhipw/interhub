# Anti AI-Looking UI Guidelines

## The Problem
Many AI-generated UIs (like those from v0, Bolt, etc.) share a common "generic" look:
- Overuse of neon/vibrant gradients.
- Excessive glassmorphism everywhere.
- Generic "inter" typography with no personality.
- Centered hero sections with floating 3D icons.
- Bombastic, non-natural copywriting.

## Our Human-Made Standards

### 1. Natural Copywriting
- **AI**: "Revolutionize your career with our cutting-edge internship platform."
- **Human**: "Bangun Karir Profesionalmu Mulai Dari Sini."
- **Rule**: Use direct, grounded, and benefit-driven Indonesian. Avoid "buzzwords" that feel translated.

### 2. Sophisticated Visuals
- **Avoid**: Rainbow neon borders or glowing buttons.
- **Do**: Use high-contrast professional palettes. Use "Primary-600" (Solid Indigo/Blue) for trust.
- **Rule**: Limit gradients to background depth, not main components.

### 3. High-Fidelity Details
- **Avoid**: Standard 8px radius.
- **Do**: Use variable radii (16px, 24px, 32px) to create an organic hierarchy.
- **Rule**: Use "Micro-interactions" (e.g., button scaling on click) that feel tactile and responsive.

### 4. Real World Imagery
- **Avoid**: Abstract 3D floating spheres or generic tech illustrations.
- **Do**: Use high-quality photography of real offices, mentors, and students.
- **Rule**: Every image must tell a story of "Magang" (Internship), not just "Tech".

### 5. Layout Complexity
- **Avoid**: Purely symmetrical, centered layouts.
- **Do**: Use split-screens, overlapping elements, and asymmetrical grids to feel "designed" by a human eye.
